🌟 More Capybara Facts

🏊‍♂️ Swimming Champions: Capybaras are excellent swimmers and can stay underwater for up to 5 minutes!

🦷 Ever-Growing Teeth: Their teeth never stop growing, so they need to keep chewing to wear them down.

🌍 South American Natives: They're found throughout South America, especially in wetlands.

👑 Rodent Royalty: As the world's largest rodents, they can weigh up to 146 pounds!

🐒 Monkey Taxis: Small monkeys often ride on their backs - it's like a free taxi service!
